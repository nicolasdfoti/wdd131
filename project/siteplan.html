Week04: Project Site Plan

Siteplan

Welcome to my siteplan website!

Here, I will tell you about my project.

Site Name

- Trackpool

This name represents a company focused on helping people and companies manage their loads.

Site Purpose

This site provides people and companies a huge opportunity: they can manage their loads and share free spaces in their trucks or vehicles. This makes it easier for them to accomodate their packages.

Scenarios

- What is the best way to move my loads or packages?

- How do I make it easier and safer to move them?

- What can I do if I have free space in my truck?

- Is it safe to share free space with others?

Color Schema

I will use at least four colors in my website:

- Space Cadet

- Battleship gray

- White

- Black

Typography

I will use Lora serif for my page

I will use it almost everywhere (header, body, footer, paragraphs, headings, etc.)
